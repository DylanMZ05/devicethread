devicethread®

Why are we different?

Imagine a world where the physical infrastructure adapts to the needs, to humans enabling unprecedented personalization, insight and efficiency. This is the world that was promised by the IoT revolution.

Previous attempts to make this happen have all fallen short because of two critical shortcomings: the choice of technology approach and how it was built.

devicethread® achieves this by creating a platform that

Abstracts and visualizes hardware as objects that can be seamlessly accessed, integrated and orchestrated together

Manifests this abstraction in the context of hospitality that makes it a seamless part of guest or staff experiences.

Surrounds this with a layer of services to provide a complete end-end solution.

devicethread® vs competitors

devicethread® takes a hotel-first approach to keyless access for next generation accommodations supporting locks of all kinds & brands. Avoid front desk lines with automated key delivery with best-in-class integration with Cloudbeds supporting all reservation scenarios.

Why devicethread® over Lynx?

Lynx is compatible with a wide range of smart devices including locks and integrates with PMSs such as Cloudbeds. It is very popular among vacation rentals and airbnb. It provides hardware & software bundled services using mostly residential smart lock devices, given they are most popular amongst its primary segment.

And as many independent hotels looked to provide Airbnb like guest experience & operational efficiencies of contactless check in, they looked at solutions like Lynx that are popular in the segment.

But this doesn't mean that Lynx is right for you, especially if you are running a hotel of any size. We know this as many frustrated hotel customers reached out to us, some even switching in the middle of peak season.

How you would benefit

• Frustrated treating each guest room of your hotel as a house with a unique street address for managing smart access

• Struggling catering to walk-in guests requiring instant key generation (situation that doesn't arise in Airbnbs and hence solutions like Lynx are not optimized)

• Figuring out what to do with access for your staff rooms or storage rooms

• Paying extra for guest messaging services that you don't use because you have another preferred vendor

• Feel stuck because your smart lock hardware credentials are not with you. Its your property and hence you own and should have to hardware like smart locks installed on your property

• You want a single stop solution for smart property infrastructure beyond access - entertainment, preventative maintenance to name a few
