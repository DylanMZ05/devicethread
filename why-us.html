devicethread®

Why are we different?

Imagine a world where physical infrastructure seamlessly adapts to human needs, delivering unprecedented levels of personalization, insight, and efficiency.

This is the future envisioned by the IoT revolution. However, past efforts have consistently fallen short due to two major shortcomings: the selection of technology and the way it was implemented.

devicethread® achieves this by creating a platform that

Abstracts and visualizes hardware as objects that can be seamlessly accessed, integrated and orchestrated together

Manifests this abstraction in the context of hospitality that makes it a seamless part of guest or staff experiences.

Surrounds this with a layer of services to provide a complete end-end solution.

devicethread® vs competitors

devicethread® takes a hotel-first approach to keyless access, designed for next-generation accommodations. We support locks of all kinds and brands, allowing you to avoid front desk lines with automated key delivery. Our best-in-class integration covers all reservation scenarios, ensuring a seamless experience.

Why devicethread® over Lynx?

Lynx is widely used in vacation rentals and Airbnbs, offering compatibility with a range of smart devices, including locks, and integrating with PMSs like Cloudbeds. It provides bundled hardware and software services, primarily using residential smart locks, which are popular in its main market segment.

As many independent hotels sought to provide an Airbnb-like guest experience and the operational efficiencies of contactless check-ins, solutions like Lynx became a consideration.

However, that doesn’t mean Lynx is the right choice for your hotel, especially if you're running a larger property. Many frustrated hotel customers have reached out to us, with some even making the switch during peak season.

How you would benefit

• Frustrated treating each guest room of your hotel as a house with a unique street address for managing smart access

• Struggling catering to walk-in guests requiring instant key generation (situation that doesn't arise in Airbnbs and hence solutions like Lynx are not optimized)

• Figuring out what to do with access for your staff rooms or storage rooms

• Paying extra for guest messaging services that you don't use because you have another preferred vendor

• Feel stuck because your smart lock hardware credentials are not with you. Its your property and hence you own and should have to hardware like smart locks installed on your property

• You want a single stop solution for smart property infrastructure beyond access - entertainment, preventative maintenance to name a few
