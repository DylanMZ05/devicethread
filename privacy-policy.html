INTRODUCTION

devicethread® enables owners and property managers of hotels, short term rentals, multi-family housing  and common work areas to monitor, secure, manage and automate the digital backbone of their Wi-Fi, networks, software applications and smart devices, all from one place (“Services”). devicethread® provides a single pane of glass view from a mobile app, table or browser across the entire digital infrastructure. devicethread® automatically secures and manages secure the digital infrastructure, allowing owners and property managers to save money through automation and ensuring a better guest experience.‍
