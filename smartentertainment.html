Smart Entertainment

Guest home entertainment experience with hospitality touch

Consumer-grade TV entertainment, seamlessly integrated into hotel operations for elevated guest experience and deeper engagement without increased costs

Key Capabilities.

Core Features

BYOC - continue watching your favorites

With seamless credential management, your guests find TV during their stay as an extension of their home devices without worrying about sign-in/sign-out complexities.

Secure

All access data is encrypted at rest or move while anonymizing PII for privacy safeguards.

Content Management

Manage content across free & payable as part of your amenities and loyalty programs

Personalized

Ability for guests to personalize what apps, contents they use, language they prefer, caption settings so that they can watch the way they do in their homes

Hardware agnostic

We work with most smart TVs in the market from box-labels to premium (Sony, Samsung, LG, Panasonic,etc.) so it works with what you have today or with what you might have tomorrow.

SmartRemote (no Apps)

Provides a simple web-based remote for each guest to intuitively access and watch contents without struggling with complex remote controls and menus.

Analytics & Insights

Access logging with analytics and insights in admin dashboard with specific recommendations for personalization and revenue upside.

How it Works

Guests

Guests share their preferences and optionally share credentials for OTT subscriptions securely with DT’s smartremote that is tied with their reservation.

Upon confirmed arrival into the room, smartremote configures guest room TV/s in real-time so that they can resume from where they left off at a power on.

Staff

Staff don’t need to anything as part of getting rooms ready for the next guest. We take care of clearing & resetting TV to pre-arrival settings automatically.

In rare cases, they have staff access to smartRemote for any troubleshooting.

Owners

A dashboard giving them 100% control for setting & managing property’s policy on a per room or collection of rooms or guest’s loyalty status. Additionally, they can manage contents from content library for their guests with ability to set payment policies.

Additionally, they receive alerts/notifications that require attention (TV going offline)
