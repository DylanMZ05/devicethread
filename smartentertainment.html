Smart Entertainment

Guest home entertainment experience with hospitality touch

Consumer-grade TV entertainment, seamlessly integrated into hotel operations for elevated guest experience and deeper engagement without increased costs

devicethread®Solutions.

Core Benefits

  • Guests

    Guests receive access credentials per hotel policy as part of their check-in process enabling a 100% contactless arrival & departure experience.

  • BYOC - continue watching your favorites

    Access guest rooms, public areas, staff-only doors, and lockers with seamless smart access—bringing convenience to all areas of your property.

  • Remote Management

    All access data is encrypted during rest or transfer, ensuring privacy and anonymity for your guests.

  • Personalized

    Let guests customize apps, content, and settings—like language preferences and captions—for a home-like viewing experience.

  • SmartRemote (no Apps)

    Guests can control TV content using a web-based remote, avoiding complicated app downloads and complex menus.

  • STAFF

    Staff can either use the codes provided by the hotel administration for access and/or use a housekeeping app with their access built into it.

  • Owners

    A dashboard giving them 100% control for setting & managing property’s policy with an ability to receive alerts, insights and on-demand reports.

  • Secure

    Robust data privacy, networking, and certifications. All data is encrypted while anonymizing personal information (PII), with constant scans to thwart unauthorized access.

  • Hardware agnostic

    Our solution works with a wide range of smart TVs, from standard to premium models, ensuring compatibility with current and future setups.

  • Analytics & Insights

    Get real-time analytics and insights with personalized recommendations to enhance guest experience and boost revenue.
