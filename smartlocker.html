Features

All-in one - guest rooms, public doors, staff-only doors

Bring smart access to public and staff-only areas as seamlessly as to the guest rooms


Mobile Key

Digital key delivered directly thru text-based message or check-in system. Regardless of how its delivered, we deliver a key - primary or back up, refreshed for every reservation


Full remote support 24/7

Single point of contact for all issues tied to access from hardware to network connectivity to software issues with remote management console to resolve issues promptly

24/7 Monitoring with real-time alerts

Be alerted to battery levels or lock going malfunctioning , real-time access alert and remote access add or removal


Integrated with Noise and Smoking monitoring

Accurate reporting of noise or smoking policy violation with integrations into reservation and front-desk operations.


Secure, and Privacy laws compliant

Entreprise grade security standards with built-in protocols for complying with stringent privacy laws ensures safety for guests and property owners
