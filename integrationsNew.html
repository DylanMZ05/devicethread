Orchestrating meaningful integrations to transform Smart Hospitality

The Smart Hospitality industry is comprised of a complex ecosystem of multiple components like IoT connectivity platforms, smart devices, property management systems (PMS) and the like. The best results can be harnessed by successfully integrating these component solutions in a way that is seamless, intuitive, simple and cost-effective.

devicethread® has thoughtfully partnered with the key players in the industry to integrate their solutions with its devicethread SmartCloud platform. The results so far have been significant and meaningful to our customers.
