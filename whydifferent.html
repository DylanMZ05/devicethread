Why are we different?

Imagine a world where the physical infrastructure adapts to the needs, to humans enabling unprecedented personalization, insight and efficiency. This is the world that was promised by the IoT revolution.

Previous attempts to make this happen have all fallen short because of two critical shortcomings: the choice of technology approach and how it was built.

01

Our approach.

Our approach is different and revolutionary! We bring together the smart objects of the physical world and combine them seamlessly into the digital world and enable a different level of hospitality to enhance the human experience - both as a guest and as a staff member.

02

Our Differentiator.

So what does devicethread® do that others, including current platform players or point business solutions have failed to do? We create an entire ecosystem that changes the paradigms of the past in how hospitality adopts smart technologies.

devicethread® achieves this by creating a platform that

Abstracts and visualizes hardware as objects that can be seamlessly accessed, integrated and orchestrated together

Manifests this abstraction in the context of hospitality that makes it a seamless part of guest or staff experiences.

Surrounds this with a layer of services to provide a complete end-end solution.

Our Vision

devicethread® was founded to solve real life challenges in the hospitality industry, today and in the future.

Our vision is to provide hospitality owners and staff a future-proof solution to efficiently and cost-effectively manage their ever-evolving smart (IoT) infrastructure and thereby deliver delightfully personalized experiences to their guests, at scale.

"Over time, the devicethread® platform will enable unification of the broader smart IoT ecosystem to include locks, thermostats, smart displays, TVs, and other sensors to enhance guest experiences.”

SWAMY VISHWANATHAN, CEO
