Capabilites


  • Usability
  • Enterprise-class
  • Smart device management
  • API-First
Simple, intuitive UI

Capabilities: Consumer-like user interface

Benefits: Easy to use by anyone in the hotel - management and staff alike

Scalability

Capabilities: Innovative concept of zones and collections which taken together enable different groupings of floors, rooms and devices across a property or campus

Benefits: A simple, elastic solution to scale properties with no restriction on # devices, property size, # device types, portfolio

Availability

Capabilities: Platform is so designed to be highly available

Benefits: Functionality can be seamlessly upgraded with no impact on the business of the property

Security

Capabilities: Constantly monitors the infrastructure to ensure all behaviors are secured

Benefits: Critical information like PII is encrypted - at rest and in motion. Devices are constantly monitored

Logging

Capabilities: All actions and changes are logged

Benefits: Automatic record keeping for business and legal compliance

Device Abstraction

Capabilities: Provides a single definition for each device type (such as lock, TV, thermostat etc), based on functional characteristics. Abstracts away the specific variations within each model and make of device.

Benefits: Allows a portfolio to be managed easily with no regard to underlying hardware variations

Role based access control

Capabilities: The platform supports a number of different roles. Different privilege levels can be associated with each of the roles

Benefits: Depending on the specific role guests, staff, managers, owners, vendors can all be given specific permissions allowing them the right access to perform their functions

Device connectivity

Capabilities: The platform has multiple means of connecting with device such as MQTT, edge drivers, on-prem SDK or cloud API service

Benefits: This layered approach simplifies platform efficiency and ensure consistent interaction with devices independent of connectivity protocol

Communication

Capabilities: Alerts and notifications are grouped into the Inbox for resolution. The platform also supports email and text notification capabilities

Benefits: Business modules can leverage the email and text notification capabilities as part of standard communication processes like notifying staff on events or delivering guest messages

API-First and webhooks

Capabilities: All functionality is accessible equivalently through programmatic interfaces as it is through the UI

Benefits: Makes it easy to integrate with any other applications to seamlessly blend the infrastructure with existing business processes
